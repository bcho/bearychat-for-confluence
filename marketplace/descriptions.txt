Create Incoming WebHook within BearyChat
-
Create a new "Incoming WebHooks" integration within BearyChat.

Copy the "Webhook URL" for configuration within Confluence.
-
You will configure which channels notifications will be posted to later on within Confluence.

---

Configure BearyChat within Confluence Admin
-
Paste the previously copied "Webhook URL" from BearyChat and hit "Save".

---

Configure Channels within Confluence Space Admin
-
Within the Confluence Space Admin, enter which channels you'd like notifications to be posted.
